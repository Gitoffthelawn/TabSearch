Privacy & Permissions

IMPORTANT

This extension uses the tabs.hide() API to filter your view. Firefox requires explicit user permission for this feature.

First Time Setup

  • The first time you hide a tab, Firefox will show a privacy dialog.
  • To use this extension, you must click Keep Tabs Hidden in that dialog.
  • This is a one-time requirement by Mozilla to ensure you are aware of the tab-hiding behavior.
Notes & Technical Details:
  • Temporary: Tabs are only hidden while searching and are restored immediately when you close the search.
  • Local Only: No data is ever sent to external servers. All matching happens entirely within your browser.
  • Workaround: The "initial hide action" setting in the popup can be disabled once you have granted permission.
MDN: tabs.hide()
