Search Tab Contents

When you enable "Search contents of loaded tabs", the extension looks beyond just titles and URLs, searching the visible text inside each open tab.

How it works

  • Precision: Matches your exact search term inside the page body.
  • Minimum Length: Requires a minimum of 3 characters to ensure performance and reduce false positives.
  • Case Insensitive: Matches "Apple" even if you type "apple".
Limitations:
  • Regular Pages: Only works on standard web pages (not about:blank, settings, or PDF views).
  • Loaded Tabs: Tabs that are currently "discarded" or unloaded cannot be searched until you click them to reload.
